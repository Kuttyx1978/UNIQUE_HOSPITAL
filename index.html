Cardiology

About the Department

Unique Hospitals is recognized as one of India's premier heart care centers. Our dedicated cardiology department is equipped with state-of-the-art Cath Labs and Cardiac Operation Theatres. We provide 24/7 emergency cardiac care, ensuring that critical patients receive immediate, life-saving interventions.

  • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)
  • Minimally Invasive Valve Replacement
  • Paediatric Heart Surgeries & Transplants

Orthopedics

About the Department

Our Orthopedics Centre of Excellence specializes in bone, joint, and spine treatments. Utilizing advanced computer-navigated surgery and modern rehabilitation protocols, we ensure patients recover faster with maximum mobility and minimal pain.

  • Total Knee and Hip Replacements
  • Sports Medicine & Arthroscopy
  • Complex Trauma & Fracture Fixation

Oncology

About the Department

The Oncology Institute at Unique Hospitals provides a multidisciplinary approach to cancer care. Our specialized tumor board—comprising medical, surgical, and radiation oncologists—works collaboratively to design personalized, targeted therapy plans for every patient.

  • Advanced Radiation Therapy (TrueBeam STx)
  • Precision Surgical Oncology
  • Chemotherapy & Immunotherapy

Nephrology

About the Department

Our Nephrology division offers comprehensive care for acute and chronic kidney diseases. With one of the largest hemodialysis units in the region and a highly successful renal transplant program, we are dedicated to restoring kidney health.

  • 24/7 Hemodialysis & Peritoneal Dialysis
  • Kidney Transplantation
  • Management of Diabetic Nephropathy

Gastroenterology

About the Department

Specializing in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of digestive tract and liver disorders. Our advanced endoscopy suites allow for minimally invasive diagnostic and therapeutic procedures with extreme precision.

  • Diagnostic & Therapeutic Endoscopy/Colonoscopy
  • Liver Disease Management & Transplants
  • Treatment of Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD)

Urology

About the Department

Our Urology department provides world-class treatment for conditions affecting the male and female urinary tract systems. We pioneer in painless, laser-based treatments for rapid recovery.

  • Laser Kidney Stone Removal
  • Prostate Enlargement Treatments (HOLEP)
  • Uro-Oncology (Prostate & Bladder Cancer)

Robotic Surgery

About the Department

Robotic-assisted surgery represents the pinnacle of modern surgical care. By utilizing the Da Vinci surgical system, our top surgeons can perform complex procedures through tiny incisions with unparalleled precision, resulting in less pain and quicker return to normal life.

  • Robotic Cardiac & Thoracic Surgery
  • Robotic Prostatectomy
  • Complex Gynecological Surgeries

Neurology

About the Department

Our Institute of Neurosciences offers advanced diagnostics and treatment for disorders of the nervous system. Our dedicated Stroke Unit ensures that patients receive 'golden hour' treatment to prevent long-term damage.

  • Acute Stroke Management
  • Epilepsy & Movement Disorders (Parkinson's)
  • Complex Brain & Spine Surgeries

Bariatric Surgery

About the Department

Our Bariatric and Metabolic Surgery center provides comprehensive weight-loss solutions. We don't just offer surgery; we provide a complete lifestyle overhaul program including nutritional counseling and psychological support to help you achieve lasting health.

  • Laparoscopic Gastric Bypass
  • Sleeve Gastrectomy
  • Post-surgery Diet & Fitness Programs
